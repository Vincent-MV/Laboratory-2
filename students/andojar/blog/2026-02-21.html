Presence

Sometimes the best way to prepare for another busy week of coding and school requirements is to step away from everything and just live in the moment. Today was one of those days for me. I spent it with my girlfriend, and honestly, that was enough.

We started by taking pictures at a photobooth. It sounds simple, but there is something special about printing memories you can actually hold. After that, of course, we ate. Food is always part of the plan. Then we went to SM and played at World of Fun. The arcade was loud and chaotic, but in a good way. For a while, I forgot about deadlines, coding errors, and unfinished tasks. I was just there, playing, laughing, and trying to win tickets.

We ended the day at their apartment just relaxing. No big agenda. No laptops open. No pressure to be productive. Just conversations, random jokes, and comfortable silence.

Days like this remind me that rest is not a waste of time. Being present with someone you care about is important too. I work hard during the week, but weekends like this make it all feel balanced.
