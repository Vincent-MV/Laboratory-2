Laying the Grounds for Personal Branding

Today was all about structural integrity. As we progress in our web systems lab, it’s become clear that we need better separation between our common styles and our personal identities. I spent a good amount of time today setting up the new portfolio/ directory properly.

One major hurdle was fixing the navigation bar so that it works seamlessly across our growing number of subdirectories. It’s always a bit of a puzzle to get those relative paths exactly right, but having a stable nav bar makes the entire user experience feel much more professional.

With the foundations now in place, I feel ready to start really honing my own personal showcase. It’s no longer just a collection of files—it’s starting to look like a brand.
