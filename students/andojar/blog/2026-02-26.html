Vibe?

Today has been a complete shift in how I look at my portfolio. I decided it was time to move past basic layouts and create something that actually feels modern. I want a premium personal portfolio that reflects who I am as a developer.

The "Vibe Coding Hub" was definitely the highlight. I wanted something unique, so I built a card that evolves when you hover over it—showing all the AI tools I use. It’s a visual way to show my toolkit, and it looks incredibly sleek. I'm really happy with how it turned out.

I also spent time fleshing out my personal info section. My goals, strengths, and even my hobbies are all laid out in these glassmorphism cards. I even decided to simplify my name to just "Vincent" in the hero section. It feels more personal and direct. Today wasn't just about code—it was about identity.
