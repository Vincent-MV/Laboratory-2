The Evolution of the Vibe Coding Workflow

Today has been an absolute game-changer for my online presence. I decided it was time to move past basic structures and into something that feels truly modern—a "premium" personal portfolio that actually reflects my personality as a Prompt Engineer.

One of the highlights of today’s work was the creation of a "Vibe Coding Hub." I wanted something unique, and with some help, I was able to build a card that literally evolves when you hover it—displaying a constellation of AI tool logos including Cursor, Gemini, Antigravity, Copilot, and Claude. It's a visual metaphor for the modern developer's toolkit and it feels incredibly sleek.

Beyond that, I spent time really fleshing out the "Personal Information" section. My new goals, strengths, and even my hobbies like gaming and weather enthusiasm are all laid out in beautifully styled glassmorphism cards. I've also moved away from using the name "Andojar" as my main identity in the hero section and shortened it to "Vincent." It feels more personal, direct, and focused. Today’s work wasn’t just about code—it was about identity.
