The Blog of the Day

Today, I pressed pause. I decided to "rewind" and strip away the noise of deadlines and digital pings. I traded my screen for the canopy of trees, finding that the sound of wind through the branches is the only playlist I really needed. By moving my body and simply being in nature, the stress that felt so heavy this morning started to neutralize. It’s a reminder that sometimes, to move forward, you first have to stand still under a tree and just breathe
